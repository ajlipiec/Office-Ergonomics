Root Ergonomics: Home and Office Consultation



What is Root Ergonomics?

Root Ergonomics is a private consultation entity located in Portland, Oregon run by Andrew Lipiec. Andrew has a Doctorate in Occupational Therapy and specializing in ergonomics. Learn About the services currently offered.



What is Ergonomics?

It's the creative science of fitting a job or task to the person instead of fitting the person to the task.

Who needs Ergonomics Consultation?

In the wake of the Covid-19 crisis, many workers are being sent home to work remotely. Often those workers do not of the tools, equipment, or educational resources nessicary to set up their offices in a function and productive format. Root Ergonomics strives to help

  • Perfroming ergonomic assessment and evaluations
  • Reccomending products to releive deficits
  • Educate on potential stretches, exericses, and strategies to prevent work-related injuries
  • Make reccomendations to alleviate developing pain related to poor ergonomics
