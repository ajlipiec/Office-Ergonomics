Root Ergonomics: Home and Office Consultation

Root Ergonomics conducts ergonomic evaluations for home and work offices. This newly established company is owned and operated by Andrew Lipiec out of Portland, OR. Andrew has a Doctorate in Occupational Therapy (OTD) and is certified as an Advanced Office Ergonomics Assessment Specialist (AOEAS).

Due to Covid-19, many individuals have been sent home to work remotely. In many cases, these individuals are equipped with inadequate office equipment and are highly prone to developing various repetative strain injuries (RSI) and other other musculoskeletal breaks downs like tendonitis and carpal tunnel syndrome. Having the correct posture, positioning, and placement of equipment can prevent and potentially fix developing breakdowns occuring during office work activties.



Consultation Options

Root Ergonomics can cater to the needs of the client. We offer either on-site evaluations, either in office or at home AND remote assessments completed via video, phone, and email interface. An assessment consists of the following:

  • Perfroming ergonomic assessment and evaluations
  • Reccomending products to releive deficits
  • Educate on potential stretches, exericses, and strategies to prevent work-related injuries
  • Make reccomendations to alleviate developing pain related to poor ergonomics
