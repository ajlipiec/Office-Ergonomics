Transformation with a certified professional at your home or office

For those in the Portland area, we have the ability to complete an in-person assessment. With this consultation, we will take measurements, conduct an in-person activity analysis, and make physical changes to your work station in the moment. Education, equipment recommendations, and stretches will be issued directly. A thorough report will be configured to review all topics discussed during the session for your reference and personal records. Due to Covid-19, ability to complete in person will be subject to current federal, state, and county rules and recommendation and use of PPE will be utilized by the evaluator per CDC guidelines.

Follow these easy steps:

Fill out and submit the following to andrew.lipiec@rootergonomics.com

  • Consent and Release of Liability
  • Client Intake Form

  • Schedule a Formal Appointment by phone or email
    • Call: (971) 386-9955
    • Click to email Root Ergonomics

    • A professional will meet you on-site to conduct the assessment and provide relevant evaluation, analysis, and education. Estimated time for assessment will be between 1 and 1.5 hours.

    A formal report reviewing information discussed during the onsite visit will be sent to you within 48-hours.

    Take up to two weeks to adjust to the discussed changes and schedule a follow up phone call for additional questions or developing concerns if needed. In-person follow-up available upon request. Additional rates may apply.

    Thrive in your newly organized work environment! Enjoy the improved productivity and decreased distractions related to cumulative strain and unnecessary repetitive movement.
