Transformation with a certified professional at your side

A video assessment is accessible by anyone throughout the United States. Rural office workers or those at home can have access to a thorough ergonomics assessment with direct education and feedback from a certified professional. This assessment progresses through the following stages, ending with a formal meeting to discuss results, questions, and provide relevant recommendations, education, and stretches. A thorough report will be configured to review all topics discussed during the session for your reference and personal records.

Follow these easy steps:

Fill out and submit the following to andrew.lipiec@rootergonomics.com

  • Consent and Release of Liability
  • Client Intake Form
  • Initial Ergonomic Measurements

Have a friend or family member take several pictures of you at your workstation. A Photo Template is available below to ensure the different relevant angle are acquired. Optionally you may upload a video of yourself completing real time work at your workstation to gather unique information and dynamic observations of your habits, posture, and position. A Video Template is available for specificification (see video below).

Call or email us to notify us when you have completed and an email will be sent with a Sync file request link to upload photos and video content. No Sync account creation needed.

Findings will be discussed with an Office Evaluation and Assessment Specialist (AOEAS) and relevant recommendations will be made. The dynamic review process will take between 45 minutes and 1.5 hours depending on your specific needs and completed using Zoom.

A formal report reviewing information discussed during the virtual visit will be sent to you within 48-hours.

Take up to two weeks to adjust to the discussed changes and schedule a follow-up phone call for additional questions or developing concerns if needed.
