Transformation with a certified professional at your side

A video assessment is accessible by anyone throughout the United States. Rural office workers or those at home can have access to a thorough ergonomics assessment with direct education and feedback from a certified professional. This assessment progresses through the following stages, ending with a formal meeting to discuss results, questions, and provide relevant recommendations, education, and stretches.

Just follow These Seven Easy Steps:

  1. Download and fill out the following documents
    • Consent and Release of Liability
    • Client Intake Form
    • Initial Ergonomic Measurements
  2. Take Workstation Pictures
    • Have a friend or family member take several pictures of you at your workstation. A Photo Template is available below to ensure the different relevant angle are acquired. Optionally you may upload a video of yourself completing real time work at your workstation to gather unique information and dynamic observations of your habits, posture, and position. A Video Template is available for specificification (see video below).
    • Photo Template
    • Call or email to notify us when completed and an email will be sent with a Dropbox file request link. No account creation needed.
  3. Schedule a Formal Appointment
    • Call: (971) 386-9955
    • Click to email Root Ergonomics
    • During that time, a certified professional will analyze your information and formulate an initial report to discuss during the meeting.
  4. Attend your Zoom meeting
    • Findings will be discussed with an Office Evaluation and Assessment Specialist (AOEAS) and relevant recommendations will be made.
    • The dynamic review process will take between 45 minutes and 1.5 hours depending on your specific needs.
  5. Receive your Personalized Report
    • A formal report reviewing information discussed during the virtual visit will be sent to you within 48-hours.
  6. Two Week Intermission
    • Take up to two weeks to adjust to the discussed changes and schedule a follow-up phone call for additional questions or developing concerns if needed.
  7. Discover your Untapped Potential
    • Thrive in your newly organized work environment! Enjoy the improved productivity and decreased distractions related to cumulative strain and unnecessary repetitive movement patterns.
