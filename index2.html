Root Ergonomics conducts ergonomic evaluations for home and work offices. Root Ergonomics is owned and operated by Andrew Lipiec out of Portland, OR. Andrew has a Doctorate in Occupational Therapy (OTD) and is certified as an Advanced Office Ergonomics Assessment Specialist (AOEAS).

Consultation Options

Root Ergonomics can cater to the needs of the client. We offer both on-site evaluations, either in office or at home AND remote assessments completed via video, phone, and email interface. An assessment consists of the following:

  • Performing ergonomic assessment and evaluation
  • Reccomending products and equipment to relieve ergonomic deficiencies
  • Education for stretches, exericses, and strategies to prevent work-related injuries as applicable
  • Make reccomendations to alleviate developing pain related to poor ergonomics
