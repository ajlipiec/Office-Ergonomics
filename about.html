Meet the Team

Andrew Lipiec OTR/L, OTD, AOEAS

Andrew Lipiec is the founder and owner of Root Ergonomics. He has a doctorate in occupational therapy and has spent the last several years specializing in hand therapy, working with patients with upper extremity injuries and disorders. His goal is to keep individuals from ever having to enter the congested world of health care by preventing musculoskeletal breakdowns before they begin. Andrew is certified as an Advanced Office Ergonomics Specialist (AOEAS) and is excited to help you achieve and sustain your work-life balance goals.
